Max Temperature

The temperature of each city showed a strong correlation to latitude, with the highest reported temperatures within 20 degrees of the equator. There are fewer cities located at extreme latitudes southe of the equator, so the plot appears to be slightly lopsided. However, if looking only at measurements between -40 and +40 degrees latitude, the plot is almost completely symmetrical with an inflection point at the equator.

The temperature correlation to latitude is a completely expected result, but still very interesting to see such a strong pattern in the data.
